GoLab Organic Molecule Builder

Introduction

Using the GoLab Molecule Builder you explore the properties and nature of 20 different molecules or compounds. This resource is spread across 4 linked sections:
  • Select a molecule and associate it with a homologous series
  • Associate the homologoue series with a functional group
  • Chose a structure map for your molecule
  • Build the molecule from various elements

Instructions

Once you have successfully completed the final section a full description of the molecule will be displayed, together with a 3D animation of the molecule. You will then be able to return to the first section and select another molecule.

Full help and instructions are shown at the start of each section. So please read these carefully before you make your choices.
